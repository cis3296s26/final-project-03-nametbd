About TalentStack

TalentStack is an AI-powered career assistant platform built for students and job seekers. It helps users manage applications, discover relevant opportunities, and stay organized throughout the job search process.

Core Features

Track Applications

Keep all your job applications from different platforms in one organized place.

AI Recommendations

Receive personalized job suggestions based on your interests, profile, and activity.

Interview Practice

Generate practice questions and prepare for interviews with smart AI assistance.

Email Updates

Stay informed with reminders and updates about your applications and next steps.
